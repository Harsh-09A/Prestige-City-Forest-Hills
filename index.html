Forest Hills - THE PRESTIGE CITY

The Prestige Group is launching Forest Hills at The Prestige City, Yogi Hills, Mulund, offering luxurious 3 and 4 BHK forest-front homes with expansive decks. This 6.5-acre serene residential phase is part of a 30-acre integrated hillside living community, providing access to essential amenities like hospitals, high street retail, cafes, restaurants, and a temple. Residents will also benefit from top-tier education with Delhi Public School located nearby. The development offers excellent connectivity, being minutes away from LBS, the Eastern Express Highway, and the upcoming Goregaon-Mulund Link Road. Forest Hills features lavish homes with expansive decks and stunning views of Yogi Hills and the Mumbai skyline. With over 50 lifestyle amenities, including pools, sports arenas, a banquet hall, and a multi-level clubhouse, it delivers an unmatched living experience. The 3BHK homes start at ₹2.82 Cr+ and 4BHK homes with two decks start at ₹5.02 Cr+. Backed by Prestige’s legacy of 300+ premium projects, including Kingfisher Towers and offices for global giants like Google and Microsoft, Forest Hills offers a blend of luxury, convenience, and exclusivity.
